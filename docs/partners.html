QualMiner: Text mining for the Venezuelan Humanitarian Response in Ecuador

Partners

They are two types of partners in the database:

  • Reporting partners: Higher level of partners reporting directly in ActivityInfo.

  • Implementing partners: Partners reporting through a reporting partner.

The table below shows the count of reporting partner per each record:

  • ACNUR has one thousand, four hundred fifty-seven records, which is 63.0% of the total records.

  • Second, NRC has one hundred seventy-eight records, which is 7.70% of the total records.

  • The most difference between percentages of the partners ACNUR and NRC is 55%.
