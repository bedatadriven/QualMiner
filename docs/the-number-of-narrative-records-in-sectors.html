QualMiner: Text mining for the Venezuelan Humanitarian Response in Ecuador

The number of narrative records in sectors

Since a robust design leads to better planning and monitoring system, it is vital to detect and understand which information is not filled by users.

If some sectors do not receive enough multi-line text data, it could be a very well indicator to make educated guesses about some things do not go well in the sectors when comes to enter long texts.

Surely, it is always better to find out the reason ‘why’; however, in some cases, removing the narrative fields from the sectors, which do not receive enough multi-text data, might also be a meaningful move.
