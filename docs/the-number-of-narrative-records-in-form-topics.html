The table shows the narrative fields with count of ’full’ and ‘empty’ fields.

  • The form Tecnico (Medios de vida y formación técnico-profesional) has 274 and 1637 records in a total of 1911 records.

  • The form VBG (Protección_VBG) has 114 and 670 records in a total of 784 records.

  • The form Educacion (Acceso_a_educación) has 63 and 517 records in a total of 580 records.

  • The form Poblacion (Manejo de la información y entrega directa de la información a la población) has 170 and 343 records in a total of 513 records.

  • The form Coordinacion (Asistencia técnica para gestion de la informacion y coordinacion) has 67 and 335 records in a total of 402 records.

Please note that the following form topics are not included in the table above because they do not contain any narrative fields: ‘Transporte humanitario’, ‘Protección general’, ‘Protección_Infancia’, ‘Protection_Otro’ and ‘Asistencia técnica para Salud’
