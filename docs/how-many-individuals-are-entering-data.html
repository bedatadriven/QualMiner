How many individuals are entering data?

We count the number of people entering data by each partner in ActivityInfo. This information might help us derive some conclusions about the responses.

Some responses would probably lead over time to more standardized responses and/or maybe less mistakes as they get to know the system/meaningful inputs vs. a more piecemeal approach (several persons entering data depending on the month and/or no dedicated resources leading to fewer inputs, especially on the narrative).

However, it is still not very much known if any organization (partner) use a kind of “common user account” to enter either all or most of the records.

In the next Response quality section, we look if there are any relationship between word counts per partner and number of people reporting to ActivityInfo.
